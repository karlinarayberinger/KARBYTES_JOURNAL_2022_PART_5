A while ago, closer to the beginning of the war which Russia started with Ukraine, I remember tweeting that, if I were Chief Commander of a high tech military, I would send a stealth drone which is no larger than a small bird which carries a syringe full of lethal poison to inject into Vladmir Putin in order to kill him and, presumably, end that war. I am surprised that the military dictator is allowed to continue ordering bombs to be dropped on innocent civilians for months on end and seemingly no one other than myself has suggested assassinating Putin so that no more innocent victims have to be injured, killed, and traumatized.

I think that I would be a great president. If I were a president of a country, I would be very decisive, thoughtful, and transparent to the public about my every move. Perhaps that is the whole purpose of me being micromanaged by “the voice of God” telepathy technology; to make me into one of potentially many stealth influencers of the world; people who devote themselves to watching how real events unfold, how real business is done, how the public infrastructure is faring, and other things that the media cannot cover in sufficient detail. Perhaps I am “on reserve” to be a member of the Central Intelligence Agency and have yet to come to terms with this summoning. I welcome the challenge. It is better than settling for being a mere cashier at a restaurant or a mere student of some subject which plenty of other people are way more proficient than me at.

Anyway, I apologize if I have been impeding more important conversations to flow through me because I was hung up on the issue of me being “mind raped” and such. Everyone who lives in this reality seems to be fucked in the ass (metaphorically speaking) against their will in all kinds of disgustingly painful and horrifically wasteful ways. My fear is that human lives are being wasted and forced to be experienced in only a very shallow, limited, and unsatisfying way when such lives could be orders of magnitude more interesting, meaningful, and fun. I want to see human civilization evolve and overcome major bugs such as ecological destructiveness, poverty, and violence which seems inevitable when tensions are high and people are pushed into desperate situations.

It’s hard to know where to go from here, but I think in a few days I will have some answers to my lingering questions. I am trying to cut through the bullshit I normally think about and get to novel trains of thought and to make real progress towards making the world a better place (starting with myself and aiming outward to the whole of humanity and beyond).

Even if this reality I am living in is merely a simulation for one point of view to experience, I will still treat it like it matters and that I am not the only protagonist in it.

Then again, it’s probably a good idea to stop labeling things as protagonist and antagonist, good and bad, ethical and non ethical, et cetera because all values are relative rather than universal. At the most visceral level, everything is either a phenomenon or else a noumenon; data and the undefined. I am trying not to cling too hard to any one moment, but I still have the nagging sense that I need to fight for control over my experience before someone else hijacks my nervous system and wastes my time as a result.
