KARBYTES_JOURNAL_2022_ENTRY_122

Around 7AM this morning, some ladies ran by me saying some insulting things about me seemingly with the intent of making me feel deprived of space and time to relax. I moved to some other spot not to far from there to continue lounging in the dirt (and, recently, I have been having trouble sleeping at night and have been mostly sleeping between 6AM and 11AM to make up for it). As soon as I got up around noon, I saw a group of ladies standing at a crossroads like they wanted to make me feel monitored. I thought I heard them say some rude things about me also. As soon as I got down to the golf course to get some water (i.e. to fill my two plastic water bottles because both were empty), there were two ladies standing by talking shit about me also. I noticed that while I was in Castro Village doing a little shopping and getting coffee, I overheard more ad hominem attacks towards me than usual. The place where I am sitting now (behind The Habit hamburger joint) is fairly secluded yet it feels very population dense and the voices which are being beamed into my skull from law enforcement are extra animosity filled, baby talky, sniveling, and plaintive compared to what I usually hear when I hang out in Dublin/Pleasanton.

(Note that the ad hominem attacks I overhear other people in my vicinity saying could possibly be superimposed over raw auditory input by law enforcement in order to prevent me from mingling with other people until I blend in better with such people).

I feel like I am getting booted off “the island” which is Castro Valley by popular demand (i.e. because many civilians want to deprive me of the niceties which Castro Valley has to offer because such people think I have had too much fun for too long and have not been punished enough for the things I have done which they consider to be morally wrong). I am hesitant to leave even though I am being tortured into leaving because Dublin/Pleasanton does not have as good of air quality as the Castro Valley region does and I like being within walking distance of the Lake Chabot wilderness (and that wilderness is quite extensive especially where that secluded little trail head begins at “The Bottom” located north of the golf course next to Redwood Road (and Redwood Road goes straight to the Laurel District of Oakland and becomes 35th avenue which goes all the way to Fruitvale BART station)).

It has been suggested to me numerous times that I am being ordered by the civilians and by law enforcement to stop talking to myself out loud and to only talk when non-anonymous people are my audience. Perhaps the torture will diminish or stop if I only speak when spoken to and otherwise keep my vocalizations to a minimum. Unfortunately, the times I have tried to be quiet, I failed within 48 hours of trying to only speak when spoken to because the bullying other people subject me to got so unbearable I felt that I needed to talk to myself out loud while walking around to release some of that pent up anger. If I did not vent out loud in the manner I have, I probably would be more creatively repressed, more depressed, and less healthy. I do not think that I should promise to shut up just to appease other people who are less concerned about environmental sustainability than I am and less open minded than I am. I think “the people” are demanding that I shut up and live on the streets outside of Castro Valley just so that those people do not have to be reminded that I have things on my mind which are not flattering of the meat eating, gasoline guzzling, anthropocentric masses. “The people” want to just settle for the predictable and myopic existence they already have planned. They do not want me to be present to remind them that they are missing out on extreme lifespan and health extension thanks to advancements in biotechnology (because such people settled for having kids and living inside of a traditionalist matrix where people are not expected to live for longer than 100 years and where people are not expected to have much to hope for other than hanging out with familiars, going on tepid vacations on Planet Earth once in a while, and going to some grindy job). I like questioning everything and coming up with novel ways to think about existence. Apparently, my creativity and willingness to hold nothing sacred rubs people the wrong way. Hence, they are effectively trying to exterminate me.

I will try not to talk out loud to myself in public from this moment onward just because I think that will reduce how much stress I am under (and my stress comes from other people harassing me and trying to torture me into compliance). I might need to go to Dublin for a few hours just to get that sniveling bride voiceover to stop being so prominent. She sounds very kid-centric and childlike herself. I am afraid that I am being prevented from thriving in Castro Valley and bullied into having to relocate to Dublin or some other place outside of Castro Valley. I feel robbed of what I feel entitled to: my homeland. I am disgusted with how kid-centric and anti-karbytes my current environment feels. I am afraid I might have to give up on making the Chabot wilderness my home (which is horrific and I am in shock about it). The cruelty of the psychotronic interference is so intense that I think that, if I stay in Castro Valley for more than a few days, I will end up being overwhelmed with anxiety, despair, and a sense that I am being singled out to be treated worse than everyone else to the extent that I vandalize property or otherwise sabotage myself.

I am headed off to Dublin now with the hopes that going there (even for just a few hours) will give me some respite from that sniveling bride voiceover and the petulance of the people around me who seem to want to make me feel deprived of room to breathe.

(That sniveling bride voice has been whining, whimpering, and trying to spoil what little enjoyment I get. Whoever condones that I be subjected to that harassment probably would say that it’s good for “the people” at my expense because this is a democracy (which means that “might makes right” and “strength in numbers” are prevailing virtues and, if the masses vote that they want one person to be tortured, then it is decidedly in humanity’s best interest to let the people indulge their sadistic urges and gang bully the loners who fail to conform to “the moral majority”).

I am still hesitant to go to Dublin just to get the bullies off my back. I’m tired of having to put myself in timeout so that people who are crueler than me, dumber than me, and more destructive to the environment than I am get to deprive me of space to peacefully inhabit my hometown.

One last thing: I am not looking for a job right now and I am hesitant to make getting a job a priority until I am confident that I have reliable “safe spaces” to go between work shifts where I can actually recuperate and enjoy my hobbies. Otherwise, taking a job would be akin to putting myself in prison. I expect to keep getting PayPal donations from my family members (and I will resort to petty theft to make ends meet regardless of whether or not anyone pays me). I will take advantage of whatever free utilities I can. By investing in my welfare and creative goals, I believe I am doing enough to contribute to society in a positive way.

It seems that, if I am the only person who wants me to achieve my goals (or if an “insufficient” number of persons want me to acieve my goals), I do not deserve to accomplish my goals because what I do matters only if a “sufficiently high” number of “sufficiently important” people decides what I do matters. That’s what makes living in Castro Valley feel so anti-karbytes to me.
