This journal entry is hopefully the last time I will ever feel the need to call attention to that ugly parasitic “ghost” of a voiceover who seems to flare up especially when I am near or at my house. I call her Pig Gorl.

Pig Gorl talks incessantly in a self righteous, baby talky, and shrill manner. The image of her face at the top of the drawing above depicts what I imagine she looks like when she has me cornered and in an uncomfortable position while she taunts me and squeals with sadistic delight. The image of her face in the middle depicts what she looks like most of the time; mumbling in a pouty manner because I am going about my business rather than lying in some catatonic state while she pokes me incessantly with invisible needles I cannot bat away because those needles are holographic yet still palpably painful and irritating. The image of her face at the bottom depicts what she looks like when I do anything remotely revolutionary. The bottom image is the rarest of her modes and does not usually last long. That is when I actually feel a bit sorry for her. That’s when she sounds like a crying baby. The middle mode is the one I am most irked by because that is the mode which sounds like she is hella more pissed off than she sounds yet she is acting like she can only take shallow breathes and whisper passive aggressively like she wants the tension to escalate into violence (because she seems to be addicted to endless violence and petulance and bickering and misery).

She exists to dampen my fun, to deter me from studying the subjects which mos pique my interest, and to keep me focused on superficial concerns. She is very sexist, shallow, limited in vocabulary, and does little more than slow down innovation and recovery. All she seems to do is add to my burdens. She does not seem to have any really usefulness. (The middle picture of her is the version of her which makes a lot of soft, plaintive hooting sounds like she is trying to get my attention while having nothing to say. Then she follows up by whispering in a hot, breathy way ad hominem attacks). I think she exists to keep me from being able to use my mind as fully as I want to and to wear me down so that I appear to be too angry and small minded for others to take seriously. The middle mode is where she often uptalks (i.e. talks in a way in which her voice sounds like it is asking a question). She tends to talk very slowly and not very clearly. She does not really enunciate consonants very well.

Pig Gorl exists to make sure that I feel harassed as often as possible so that other people who are deemed more important than me get to feel more welcome wherever I am. Pig Gorl exists to drive Karlina into self destructive behavior for the amusement of privileged others. If enough of such people want to get rid of me, Pig Gorl will do an effective job of harassing me to the point that I am forced to leave the premises, postpone my plans indefinitely, and, as a last resort, kill myself. She is a solution to the problem of overpopulation in a world where almost all adults are implicitly coerced into birthing as many offspring as possible. Some people have to be exterminated to make way for baby. There ain’t nuttin’ I can do about it! The “moral majority” seem to like that they personally do not have to murder me nor maim me. If they don’t like me, all they gotta do is call up Pig Gorl and I am beaten up on their behalf. Then those “moral majority” can fan themselves and coo at what good citizens they are.

Please note that all of the journal entries in this chapter so far are a bit hyperbolic, one-sided, and not very relevant to my interests overall. I just wanted to get these things out of the way so that I do not have to keep them bottled up inside like a ticking time bomb ready to explode at an inopportune time. I want the world to know that I feel harassed very often and treated like I deserve to be treated worse than almost everyone else around me. It is quite alienating and it makes me reluctant to “play the game” others want me to; the game where I have to conform to what other people think is normal so that I can get a job and perhaps mitigate some of the suffering I experience as a result of being so frequently harassed and worn down. I can tell that other people are not ready to treat me as anything more than a delinquent child to ignore and bully until I bend over backwards to ingratiate myself to them and to be their little cuck. Though I may not like how I am treated in this society, I have to put up with this “tough love” because I have no other choice.
