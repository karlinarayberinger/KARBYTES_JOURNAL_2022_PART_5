KARBYTES_JOURNAL_2022_ENTRY_137

After being talked to in a derogatory manner so damn frequently for so damn long by those fucking voices which I have attempted to describe in as much detail as I could throughout the lifespan of Karbytes For Life dot WordPress dot Com, I started talking back out loud rather than continue trying to keep my mouth shut and my voice silent while those bullies needle me incessantly and drown out my thoughts with their banal, depressing, and wasteful subject matter. I told them that no matter how hard I try to be on my best behavior and to please them and/or others, my efforts are mostly unappreciated and the bullying does not seem to get better. The voices seem obsessively fixated on what I do with my mouth. It seems that the voices are not interested in what I have to say when I talk out loud. They only seem to care that my mouth is opening and my lips are moving (and I deliberately try to make my lips easy for drones to read because pretending I have stealth drones watching me from the sky makes me feel less alone in this war). The voices seem to have an issue with my mouth not being sealed shut because, if my mouth is moving freely and making shapes which do not conform to what it means to be in a straitjacket of some sort, that means that I am getting too much free time to pay attention to the content of my mind (which is a sin in a world which implicitly requires that everyone be preoccupied with what the government deems to be safe thoughts only).

I heard the voices say, “Get a job,” and I felt dread at the pit of my stomach lurch upwards like liquid from a black lagoon of doom and fear. I got a montage of morbid flashbacks of the kinds of menial jobs I have done over the years.

Without belaboring my point, “self” seems to be the dirtiest word in the dictionary these days. It is seemingly not okay for a person to merely take care of itself. A person is required to participate in some kind of community and to be controlled by that community. Otherwise, that person is a nuisance and gets treated as a pest to be removed and, if possible, reformed into a less individualistic person and a more communal person.

I apparently do not have enough to talk about with prospective friends and coworkers. Some things I think I could talk about with my future acquaintances are common interests such as art, drugs, and camping but that’s about it. There is not much more I feel I can offer as conversation fodder which would appeal to people I barely know and will likely never get to know on a deeper level than just “on the clock” socializing. (Some people may protest that I am setting the bar too low by insinuating that I am considering applying for jobs in a fast food restaurant or retail outlet instead of aiming higher and going for an office job. Perhaps I secretly like not having to be very high up on the corporate ladder. Perhaps I like being cheap and having lots of free time and not having to work very hard to make money).

Anyway, I would like to suggest that it is indeed possible for a human being to thrive without much in the way of a social network so long as that person has good enough things to keep that person preoccupied mentally. I do not think that loneliness and depression are inevitable consequences of being hermetic. Instead, I think that people become depressed and lonely because they have been indoctrinated to assume that being socially isolated is directly ruinous to their health and that The Meaning of life is found primarily in human-to-human relationships. I think that, with enough deep introspection and perhaps some strong enough psychedelics, a human being can transcend that brainwashing and discover that all things are connected and that separation from others is an illusion. That realization has helped me not be as needy as I otherwise would be for other people’s affection, attention, and investment. It has also made me more compassionate towards everyone (especially people who annoy me and people who I think are hideously cruel and destructive).

Anyway, the sky is getting dark. I am sitting on that hill between Castro Valley and Hayward after picking up some cannabis pills from the local weed shop. After charging my electronics at the church (if that’s available; otherwise, my dad’s house), I plan to hike up to that bathroom up at Lake Chabot campgrounds to use my laptop in a sheltered, quiet place. I have not been up there in about a week. I miss it. Well, time to go!

By the way, it really cheered me up today when some familiar people told me that they appreciate me and smiled when I showed up. I was having a hard time before I arrived and that significantly made my day better. Sometimes I forget what it feels like to interact with nice, professional people. I spend too much time in rough places where I hear little more than mean words. I do not want to become mean as a result of absorbing too much of that Debbie Downer crap. I will try to remember the nice examples and go out of my way to find inspiring people online and in the physical world.

Thank you for being patient with me as I make sense of what is going on in my mind and as I build this website cluster into an increasingly sensical map of my reality. I am optimistic that the content and quality of my life will improve as I age (and if and when I get to an age where I start to seriously fall apart and lose my physical and mental abilities, I expect to have a decent philosophy which will help me fall apart and die with dignity and humor).
