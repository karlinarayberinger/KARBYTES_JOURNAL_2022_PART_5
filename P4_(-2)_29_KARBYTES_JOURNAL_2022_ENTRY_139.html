KARBYTES_JOURNAL_2022_ENTRY_139

It is approximately 11:23AM. I am sitting in my backyard (i.e. my dad’s house on the northeast side of Castro Valley) sipping coffee and sitting on a plastic crate which is just now bowing under my gargantuan weight. I had some interesting dreams last night and this morning. One interesting detail was being inside of my dad’s hosue but only that house grew to the size of a very large castle and included many random residents and even that church I was sleeping at a few blocks downhill and south of my actual house. Some voiceover said, “One big happy home,” and yes, I do recall being happy during that scene. I was happy to open my concept of what a home is to make the entire universe my home (and to think of every other creature as my housemate).

I would like to admit that I am embarassed about many of the pink bin pages I have published. I a lot of what I said in the pink bin is hyperbolic, reactionary, and ill informed; not things I am proud of nor want to uphold as examples of how I currently see the world. I will just leave whatever I already published in tact for future reference (specifically so that I can compare how badly I was doing during the pink bin phase compared to how well I expect and hope to be doing forever afterwards).

I used to have a YouTube channel with short videos of natural phenomena such as waves crashing against the beach and videos of me playing the drums. I have not made a drumming video in months but have been practicing at least once per week for at least twenty minutes. I think my drumming skills have improved. The reason why I deleted my YouTube channel was to prevent people from seeing “related videos” in the side bar which revealed some embarassing browswing history. I would rather not let the general public see my web browsing history because I do not want to be ridiculed.

I kept the videos backed up to my account with Archive dot org. Sadly, after getting bullied mercilessly by a voiceover yesterday, I deleted all videos and some valuable posts from my Instagram because I did not want to be ridiculed. I removed any content which I thought made me easy to ridicule as a feminazi or try hard such as posts about birth control and all videos. Today I hope to get my first drumming video in a while back up on the Instagram because, without those drumming tiles, the collection seems to be missing a particular kind of charm I am nostalgic about; the feeling of infinite summer and sprawling northern California wildnerness as my backyard. My cat Brea looks like a model citizen of that universe. She is depicted as living both indoors and outdoors. (In real life she has a cat door which allows her to enter and exit the house on her volition).

Anyway, I am glad that I lowered my standards in terms of what I plan to be doing to make some money for security’s sake: part time retail and/or food service. I will just pretend that I am a community college student looking for a part time job while I “go to school” so that I can blend in better with society and get more perks out of it without having to be such a try hard like I was when I thought I had to be a white collar employee to survive. I realize that I can seriously keep living out of my back (i.e. the camper’s lifestyle) and make computer programming, psychedelic trips, and random Internet browsing my main hobbies. I can take my sweet merry time because I cannot be evicted from the Bay Area. I can pretty much live wherever I want as a vagrant.

Anyway, I want to say that I am very grateful for my life and for all the persons in it and that I want to be on good terms with everyone. Whatever transgressions anyone (myself included) may have committed in the past I want to now just forgive and not spend any more time dwelling on so that I can just make the most of what I have and not squander this lifetime.

One last thing, Pig Gorl voice does not seem to get better with time. She is just as annoying as ever. I have decided to stop trying to justify her presence as useful after seeing how she seems to have no purpose other than sabotaging my fun and coercing me into spending myself broke to cope with what a difficult bitch she is. I am a kind person, but if I had the opportunity to quickly murder her and get away with it, I probably would. I would not hesitate to kill people who I am convinced hold humanity back from evolving and being healthy and happy. Those who want to hold others back are parasites which negatively contribute to society. Hence, exterminating them would be in society’s best interest. I do believe some murder is ethical.

Yeah, I think Pig Gorl is jealous of me for having a nice place to live, supportive family members, a brilliant mind, and lots of things to look forward to. I do not feel sorry for her like I used to. Instead I feel disgusted by her the way I am disgusted when I see a piece of shit on the ground. I do believe that Pig Gorl is a standin for many people who resort to “lose lose” conflict mongering and sabotage. Such people are not very fortunate nor are they very educated. Such people are so mentally stunted that they default to being humorless, catty, ad hominem, unimaginative, depression mongering dead weight which I know in my heart and soul would be best replaced by robots at whatever their paid jobs are. If I could, I would pay those people to just do whatever they want (on a fixed budget, of course) while robots take over their jobs and the people those robots get replaced by would get paid for the robots’ labor (and the robots need no health insurance, the robots do not have to go on strike, and the robots are always ready to work at full capacity without complaint).

Perhaps if I keep punching the walls of the matrix enough, a door will open and all everyone in my universe will have access to Infinite Summer. At the very least, it is a nice fantasy to savor while grinding away in a sweatshop for a modicum of pay while my body and mind gradually atrophy. I am more okay with the prospect of my own decay and death than ever before. That does not mean I will not take every measure I can to extend my lifespan and capabilities.
